Sound Comparisons: West Papua

From 2019 to 2020, Sound Comparisons hosted a West Papua component. As of December 2020, the data are now maintained at Papuan Voices.

To ensure that the former Sound Comparisons: West Papua remains citeable as such, below is a record of the online publication details from the original ‘how to cite’ page. (To cite instead the Papuan Voices app, see here.)
