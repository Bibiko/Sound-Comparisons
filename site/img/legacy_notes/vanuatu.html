Sound Comparisons: Vanuatu

From 2016 to 2020, Sound Comparisons hosted a Vanuatu component. As of December 2020, the data are now maintained at Vanuatu Voices.

To ensure that the former Sound Comparisons: Vanuatu remains citeable as such, below is a record of the online publication details from the original ‘how to cite’ page. (To cite instead the Vanuatu Voices app, see here.)

Cite As:

Shimelman, Aviva, Paul Heggarty, Giovanni Abete, Laura Wägerle, Darja Dërmaku-Appelganz & Mary Walworth. 2019. Sound Comparisons: Vanuatu. https://soundcomparisons.com/Vanuatu
