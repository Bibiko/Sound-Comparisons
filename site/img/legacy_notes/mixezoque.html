Sound Comparisons: Mixe-Zoque

From 2019 to 2020, Sound Comparisons hosted a Mixe-Zoque component. As of December 2020, the data are now maintained at Mixe-Zoque Voices.

To ensure that the former Sound Comparisons: Mixe-Zoque remains citeable as such, below is a record of the online publication details from the original ‘how to cite’ page. (To cite instead the Mixe-Zoque Voices app, see here.)

Cite As:

Kondić, Ana, Darja Dërmaku-Appelganz & Paul Heggarty. 2019. Sound Comparisons: Mixe-Zoque. https://soundcomparisons.com/MixeZoque
